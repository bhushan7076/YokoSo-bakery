About Yokoso Bake House

Japanese-inspired bakery in Pune

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Yokoso Bake House began as a passion project bringing authentic Japanese baking techniques to Pune. What started as a small home bakery has grown into a beloved local destination for those seeking light, fluffy cheesecakes and delicate pastries with a Japanese twist.

Our name "Yokoso" means "welcome" in Japanese - and that's exactly how we want every customer to feel when they visit us.

Our Baking Philosophy

Quality Ingredients

We source only the finest ingredients, from premium Japanese matcha to locally-sourced dairy.

Authentic Techniques

Our bakers train in traditional Japanese methods to create light, airy textures.

Creative Fusion

We blend Japanese baking traditions with Indian flavors for unique creations.
